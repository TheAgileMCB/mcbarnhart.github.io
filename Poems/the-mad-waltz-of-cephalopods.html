Jupiter Appears to Jesus in the Garden of Gethsemane

These small and significant shells dance the waves
like green buoys afloat in the viscous Dead Sea
with no drive, with no purpose, no goal, but design.
And they color the sea with their ink black as tar
like from crude fountain pens, and their disco bright shells
play bright flashes of rainbow on white ocean mist,
and it sparks in the heat of the sun over head
like a volatile brew of tart carbon, methane,
and grim oxygensbane. Light and dark tendrils span
the tall waves like foul pools between water and sky
and draw glass and ersatz metal stones to their sides
As they float, huddled mass, aimless horde, hot and dead.

With eight hands in eight and a poison in each
in small vials that kill like the Exxon Valdez
in a waltz, a crescendo of gathered debris,
they are there when the ebb-tide pulls more out to sea
to connect in the dance to its lead on the beat
and it spins and it twirls without watching its feet
and it moves with no grace but with blundering speed,
each new partner ensnared with an oily tight cling
and compelled forth to whirl and to jive and to swing!

What could imagine the exotic scenes
in the gyre, the dance of these synthetic beings
as they reach and they grip and they kill and they bring
Bird-brained life and sick fish to a pained and cold brink
through near-yards of fresh water, O, what toxic scenes
have these small and significant cephalopods seen?
